Say goodbye to expired food in your fridge

Everything is temporary, including food. This unfortunate fact is demonstrated by expiration dates that are stamped on perishable foods that we buy and store in our refrigerator. We want to revolutionize the way grocery shopping is done by introducing a device that alerts you if something in your fridge is about to expire and then delivering the required items to you within an hour.
